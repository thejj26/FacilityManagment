Welcome

Welcome to Facility Management, your go-to tool for analyzing facility finances. Enjoy an easy-to-use interface that includes everything you need - no clutter, just efficiency.

About us

Created as a graduation project by Jakov Jozić and Luka Morić, Facility Management is a web-based tool that helps users manage facility finances by providing an overview of the costs and earnings of their facilities.

About us

How it works

It's simple! Once you create an account, add your facility (or facilities) on the Facility Overview page. Then, input all the running costs and earnings you've received. Facility Management handles all the calculations for you, displaying the results in a graphical overview for each facility. It really is that easy!
